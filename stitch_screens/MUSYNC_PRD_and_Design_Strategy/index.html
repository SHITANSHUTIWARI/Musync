# MUSYNC - Product Requirements & Design Strategy ## 1. Product Vision MUSYNC is a professional networking and collaboration platform for music creators. It avoids social media pitfalls (likes, followers, virality) and focuses on meaningful professional partnerships. ## 2. Design Philosophy - **Structure:** LinkedIn (Professional, organized, networking-focused). - **Aesthetic:** Spotify (Dark mode, immersive, music-centric). - **Quality:** Linear/Apple (Precise spacing, premium typography, subtle motion). ## 3. Core User Flows 1. **Onboarding:** Signup/Login flow. 2. **Discovery:** Searching for artists and projects via a high-fidelity Discover page. 3. **Identity:** A world-class Profile page acting as a professional resume for artists. 4. **Collaboration:** Project management and professional Chat UI. ## 4. Technical Objectives - **Frontend Architecture:** Clean, modular, and responsive. - **UI/UX:** Premium animations (Framer Motion), skeleton loaders, and robust error handling. - **Layout:** Sidebar-driven dashboard with a consistent 12-column grid.
